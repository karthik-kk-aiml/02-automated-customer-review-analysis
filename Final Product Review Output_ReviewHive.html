Fire HD 8

General Summary:

Amazon Fire 8 inch tablet is great for e-reading on the go, nice and light weight, and for the price point given, definitely worth the purchase. Children love to use it...

Highlights:

Amazon Fire 8 is a great value for the price. Great for e-reading on the go, easy to use, and great for traveling.

Issues:

Can't remove any of the Amazon apps. The apps take up too much of the memory. Also can't use apps from non-Amazon sources unless rooted.

Kindle

General Summary:

The Kindle Oasis is very tiny, portable & fits in about anything from a jeans back pocket to a very small purse. Can read without annoying seatmate with overhead seat light...

Highlights:

The Kindle Oasis is the smallest of all the Kindle products. However, the WiFi connectivity is patchy at best.

Issues:

A three-year-old Kindle outperformed the Oasis. Battery life is better on the older device, and the magnetic connector is poorly designed.
